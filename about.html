About Eliezer

Our story, our passion

Who We Are

Eliezer is a passionate jewellery brand from Kerala, crafting timeless pieces that celebrate beauty, tradition, and elegance. Every piece tells a story of love and craftsmanship.

Our Mission

To bring affordable luxury jewellery to every home, combining traditional Kerala craftsmanship with modern designs that suit every occasion.

Why Choose Us

100% certified gold and diamonds. Handcrafted by skilled artisans with over 20 years of experience. Every piece comes with a quality guarantee.
